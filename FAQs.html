Frequently asked questions

What services does your company offer?

At Bueno Engineering, we specialize in a range of industrial fabrication and engineering services. Our expertise includes rubber glove line fabrication, steelwork for tanks and structures, utilities engineering, machinery and equipment fabrication and installation, as well as mechanical, electrical, and instrumentation (MEI) services.

How can I partner with your company?

If your company offers products or services that align with our areas of expertise, such as rubber glove manufacturing equipment, steel fabrication, utility solutions, industrial machinery, or MEI services, we welcome partnership inquiries. Simply fill out the form on our "Become a Partner" page, and our partnership team will review your submission and reach out to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

What geographical regions do you serve?

While we are based in Malaysia, our services are not limited by geographical boundaries. We have experience working with clients both locally and internationally, and we are equipped to undertake projects in various regions around the globe.

Can you handle custom fabrication projects?

Yes, we excel in providing customized solutions to meet the specific requirements of our clients. Whether you need a bespoke steel structure, a tailored machinery fabrication, or a specialized utilities engineering solution, our team of experts is ready to collaborate with you to bring your vision to life.

How do I request a quote for a project?

To request a quote for your project, simply reach out to us via email or of our social media. Provide us with as much detail as possible about your project requirements, and our team will promptly respond with a customized quote tailored to your needs.

Are your services scalable to accommodate projects of different sizes?

Yes, our services are scalable to accommodate projects of varying sizes and complexities. Whether you require a small-scale equipment installation or a large-scale industrial fabrication project, we have the capabilities and resources to meet your needs.
